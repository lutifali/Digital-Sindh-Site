About Us

Shafique Ahmed Unar

CEO & Founder of the Digital Sindh Institute of Management & Technology

CEO Message

The mission of the Digital Sindh Institute of Management & Technology (DSIMT) is to empower individuals with cutting-edge digital education and skills, fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and leadership. We are committed to delivering high-quality, industry-relevant programs, contributing to the development of a digitally skilled workforce, and advancing technological solutions that benefit society

Our vision at DSIMT is to be a global leader in digital education and technology-driven solutions. We aspire to create a dynamic learning environment that nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning. Through strategic partnerships with industry leaders and a commitment to research and innovation, we aim to shape the future of digital excellence in Sindh and beyond.

Khalil Ahmed Unar

Director of the Digital Sindh Institute of Management & Technology

Director Message

Our institute is at the forefront of technological innovation and education in Sindh, and our mission is to prepare our students for success in a rapidly evolving digital world. We understand that technology is transforming every aspect of society and the economy, and we are committed to equipping our students with the knowledge and skills they need to thrive in this digital era.

Head of the Institute Message

Dear Young Generation,
"In today's world, technology offers an unprecedented gateway to knowledge and creativity. Harness its power to access a vast repository of information through online courses and digital libraries. Develop critical thinking skills to evaluate the information you encounter, ensuring you can distinguish fact from misinformation. Embrace your role as digital natives by learning to code and create, whether it's building software, designing websites, or expressing your creativity through digital art and music. Stay globally connected, engaging with diverse perspectives and cultures. Above all, remember to use technology ethically, striking a balance between your digital life and the real world, while continuously adapting and evolving in this ever-changing technological landscape."
